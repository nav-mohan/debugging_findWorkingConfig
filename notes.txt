## how to do adaptive search
	if you pick the left-most i.e min_index < 4 
		and if there are no further local minimas 
			then expand the search further left 
		and there are other local minimas 
			then pick the first local minima to the right 

	if you pick the right most i.e min_index > len(valid_alats)-4
		and if there are no furthe local minimas 
			then ignore this species. this case is unlikely because we are searching all the way to 12 angstronms
		and if there are other local minimas 
			then pick the first local minima to the left
